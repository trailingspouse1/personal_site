Diana Mahmoud created BeakSpeak to help business owners who feel intimidated by the ever-changing digital world to have both the knowledge and confidence to improve their online presence and customer engagement. “I understand not everyone grew up with an iPad because I didn't either. As much as I hate to admit it, I took a typing class instead of a keyboarding class. I also remember a time when a mouse was to be loathed, cookies were to be eaten, and browsing was done in public at the local mall.”

Despite generational disadvantage, she built a career, first in traditional media and later in digital with a focus on customer engagement and online user experience. As an accomplished multimedia project manager with SAS’s award-winning Curriculum Pathways,a interactive educational software, she led the development of cutting-edge Internet content. Today, she uses her experience and expertise as a certified social media marketer to help others understand how to use the latest tools to promote themselves and their businesses.
